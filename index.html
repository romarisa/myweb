What can I do for you

🎯

User Experience (UX) Design

Designing user-centered interfaces through research and needs analysis. Creating wireframes, prototypes, and usability testing to ensure an intuitive and effective user experience.

🖼

User Interface (UI) Design

Developing visually appealing and functional interfaces using modern design principles. Includes creating graphic elements, color palettes, typography, and responsive design for various platforms.

📂

Design Systems

Creating and maintaining custom design systems to ensure consistency and efficiency in digital product design. Includes style guides, component libraries, and reusable design patterns.

👓

Web Accessibility

Auditing and optimizing websites to meet accessibility guidelines (WCAG). Implementing practices to ensure content is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities..

📊

User Research and Usability Testing

Conducting research to understand user needs and behaviors. Performing usability tests to validate designs and improve user interactions with the product.

🪄

Redesign of Existing Interfaces

Updating and improving existing user interfaces to optimize user experience and visual performance. This may include modernizing appearance, enhancing usability, and implementing new features.
