About Me

I'm Roselly, born and raised in Peru 🇵🇪, where my love for drawing as a child led me to pursue graphic design. I studied at art school, where I was immersed in creativity and artistic expression 🎨. After working for years as a graphic designer, I moved to Barcelona to pursue a Master's in UX/UI Design at Elisava, and then I fell in love with the city's vibrant cultural life ✨.

I've spent more than 5 years working as a UX/UI designer, and I'm continuously improving my development skills, which you can see through this very website 👩🏻‍💻!

I bring empathy, enthusiasm, and adaptability to every project. Whether working solo or collaborating with a team, I thrive on helping others and learning something new every day. My life is guided by freedom, perseverance, and the joy of growth 🙌.

Now, after several years here, I've made Barcelona my home 🏡. One of my favorite activities is co-hosting an improvisation meetup in Ciutadella Parc 🌱. It's a great way to connect with others and get inspired. (By the way, if you are in Barcelona, you're welcome to come. More info in this meetup page)
